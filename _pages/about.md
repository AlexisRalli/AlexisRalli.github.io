Hi, I'm Alexis Ralli a quantum chemistry PhD student in the [Centre for Computational Science group (CCS)](https://ccs.chem.ucl.ac.uk/) at [University College London (UCL)](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/). Currently, my work focuses on applications of quantum computing to chemistry. In particular I am studing the variational quantum eigensolver (VQE) algorithm. 
 
I earned an MChem in chemistry from the [University of Manchester](https://www.manchester.ac.uk/), followed by an MRes in quantum technology from [UCL](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/).
